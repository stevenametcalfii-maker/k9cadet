Comprehensive Training Programs

Mobility Assistance

Specialized training for dogs supporting individuals with physical disabilities. Our mobility assistance dogs are trained to help with daily tasks, provide balance support, and enhance independence.

Duration: 18-24 months | Certification: EU Standard

Medical Alert Dogs

Advanced training for detecting medical emergencies and providing alerts. These dogs can detect changes in blood sugar, oncoming seizures, and other medical conditions before they become critical.

Duration: 12-18 months | Certification: EU Standard

Psychiatric Service Dogs

Comprehensive training for mental health support and crisis intervention. These dogs provide comfort, security, and assistance for individuals with PTSD, anxiety, depression, and other psychiatric conditions.

Duration: 15-20 months | Certification: EU Standard

Custom Programs

Tailored training solutions for specific individual needs. We work closely with clients to develop personalized training programs that address unique requirements and challenges.

Duration: Variable | Certification: EU Standard
